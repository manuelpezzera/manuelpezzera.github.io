My Games & AR/VR Apps

Tinalp

2021 - Current

Tinalp is a learning platform which revolutionizes the concept of education 4.0 introducing augmented classroom which simulates practical scenarios, with real-time trainer-trainees interaction, for a customized and immersive learning experience.

I co-developed the versions for Hololens, NReal Air, ARCore and Oculus Quest.

Zero

2015 - 2020

Zero mixes strategic, tower defense and FPS elements. Build your empire: face many challenges and find the right strategy to win. You are free to download and try the first Zero demo.

I have been responsible for the following tasks:
  • Core mechanics
  • User Interface & User Experience
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Audio & sounds effect programming
Developed with the collaboration of Alberto Clarari and Federico Spinardi.

Video games for rehabilitation

2017 - Current

Part of the work done during the doctoral program. I contributed to the development of a suite of exer-games designed to enable patients with multiple sclerosis to perform autonomous home rehabilitation. My work also includes an online platform to support clinicians in monitoring patient progress.

2014-2017

Design and development of several public transportation applications for Windows & Windows Phone platforms. Over 60,000 downloads and 3,000 positive reviews (i.e., at least 4 stars). I took care of both the mobile part (C# & XAML) and the server part (PHP & SQL).

About Me

I have a Ph.D. in Computer Science, and for the last 8 years I have been working mainly on game development. In the past, I also covered mobile app development. Always been passionate about computer science, I've been programming since I was 16 years old. Graduated with honors, alongside my university studies I have always carried out complete autonomy or with a small group of colleagues, personal projects, first mobile applications, then video games. My passion for games and research then led me to pursue a PhD focused on the design and development of video games applied to rehabilitation.
